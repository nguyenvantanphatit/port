### Overview
**Echo Medi Mental Health Platform** is a modern web application designed to make mental health services more accessible. It allows users to **book therapy sessions**, **take psychological assessments**, and **read curated articles** by mental health professionals. The platform emphasizes simplicity, usability, and local integration to support users on their wellness journey.

### Key Features
- **Appointment Booking**: Users can browse available time slots, choose a preferred date and time, and book a consultation session with a licensed therapist.
- **Psychological Assessments**: The platform provides self-assessment tools for MBTI, stress, depression, anxiety, and more, with results displayed instantly.
- **Expert Articles**: Users can read insightful, expert-written articles covering mental health, emotional well-being, and personal development strategies.

- **Frontend**: Next.js with the App Router, Tailwind CSS, and Shadcn UI components.
